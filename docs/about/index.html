Yashaswi Nursery


Welcome to Yashaswi Nursery, Puttur. We make premium grafts of cashew, pepper, and seedlings of coconut and arecanut. Please click any of the plants below for more details.

Yashaswi Nursery is located within the lush greens of Western Ghats, about 50 km from the coastal town of Mangalore. It was founded by Dr. N Yadukumar and Smt. Vanitha N Y in 2012, a former research scientist of Directorate of Cashew Research (DCR), Puttur.
